Angina or Heart Attack

IMPORTANT

Questionnaire to Determine Probable Angina, Heart Attack, Stroke or Transient Ischemic Attack.

1. Have you felt any pain or discomfort, or any pressure or heaviness in your chest? Nakakaramdam ka ba ng pananakit o kabigatan ng iyong dibdib?

2. Do you feel pain in the center or left chest or left arm? Ang pananakit ba ay nararanasan sa gitna o kaliwang bahagi ng dibdib, o sa kaliwang braso?

3. Do you feel chest pain when you're walking uphill or in a hurry? Nararamdaman mo ba ito kapag ikaw ay nagmamadali o naglalakad nang mabilis o paakyat?

4. Do you sit down when you feel the pain while walking? Tumitigil ka ba sa paglalakad kapag sumasakit ang iyong dibdib?

5. Does the pain go away when you stand still or when you place a tablet under your tongue? Nawawala ba ang sakit kapag ikaw ay hindi kumilos o kapag naglagay ka ng gamot sa ilalim ng iyong dila?

6. Does the pain go away in less than 10 minutes? Nawawala ba ang sakit sa loob ng 10 minuto?

7. Have you ever had a severe chest pain which lasted for half an hour or more? Nakakaramdam ka ba ng pananakit ng dibdib na tumatagal ng kalahating oras o higit pa?

Note: If the answer is YES to either questions 3, 4, 5, 6, or 7, the patient may have angina or heart attack, and must see a doctor.

Stroke or Transient Ischemic Attack

IMPORTANT

Questionnaire to Determine Probable Angina, Heart Attack, Stroke or Transient Ischemic Attack.

8. Have you experienced any of the following:

  • Difficulty in talking.
  • Weakness of arm and/or legs on one side of the body or numbness on one side of the body?

Have you experienced any of the following:

  • Hirap sa pagsasalita.
  • Panghihina ng braso at/o ng binti o pamamanhid ng kalahating bahagi ng katawan?

Note: If the answer to question 8 is YES, the patient may be having TIA or stroke, and must see a doctor.
